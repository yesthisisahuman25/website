Recipe: Underwater biscuit

Equipment:

  • Preserving Pan
  • Spoon
  • Knife
  • Hammer

Ingredients:

  • Sugar
  • Strawberries
  • Blueberries
  • Raspberries
  • Blackberries
  • Barnacles
  • Bee Stingers

Steps:

  1. First mask all your berries with the hammer and put them in the preserving pan
  2. Put the sugar in and mix them with a spoon
  3. Then set the pot for 500 degrees and 1 minute
  4. Then put bee stingers in there then your done
