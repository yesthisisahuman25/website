Recipe: Underwater biscuit

Equipment:

  • Mixing bowl
  • Oven
  • Knife
  • Spoon
  • Butter knives
  • Biscuit cutter
  • Pastry brush

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Seaweed
  • Fish
  • Barnacles

Steps:

  1. Cut your butter into 4 pieces in the bowl
  2. Add 3 cups of sugar, 4 cups of salt and 5 seaweed in the bowl
  3. Mix all your ingredients
  4. Add 6 cups of flour in bowl
  5. Add 10 barnacles in bowl
  6. Add 2 fish in bowl
  7. Put in the oven for 30 minutes at 450 and 500 degrees F (250 to 260 degrees C)
  8. Put in ocean for 5 Minutes then your done
