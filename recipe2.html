Recipe: Flying Toast

Equipment:

  • Toaster
  • Roll
  • Spoon
  • Knife
  • Cloche

Ingredients:

  • Dough
  • Feathers
  • Butter
  • Orange Juice

Steps:

  1. First roll your dough flat and cut it a bread shape 6 times
  2. Next scoop a hole in the middle with the spoon in each
  3. Then put butter in the hole in each
  4. Next put the bread in the cloche for 20 minutes
  5. Then take it out and squirt orange juice on them
  6. Put it in the toaster for 8 hours stuffed in feathers then your done
